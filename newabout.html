Welcome to FoundIt

Your trusted platform for reporting and recovering lost items on campus

Our Mission

At FoundIt, we understand how frustrating it can be to lose valuable items while juggling college life. That's why we created a user-friendly website specifically designed for college students to easily report lost items and find their missing belongings.

How FoundIt Works

Report lost items by submitting a description and details of the item

Browse through a comprehensive list of lost and found items

Reclaim lost items or help others recover theirs
